FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

What ages do you train?
We work with athletes from ages 6 through adulthood. Youth, middle school, high school, and adult personal training are all available.
Where are sessions held?
Sessions take place at our training facility. See the Location section below for the full address and directions.
How long is each session?
Sessions are available in 30-minute ($40), 45-minute ($55), and 60-minute ($65) formats depending on the program and athlete.
How is availability determined?
A combination of online scheduling and in-person scheduling. Training schedules are formed weekly — contact Bastian to confirm open slots for the coming week.
Do you offer group or team training?
Yes — team packages are available with custom pricing. Contact Bastian directly to discuss your team's needs.
What should my child bring?
Athletic clothing, sneakers (or cleats for field work), a water bottle, and any sport-specific gear (glove, bat, etc.). If equipment is forgotten, Bastian has gear on hand to use for the session.
What's your cancellation policy?
We ask for at least 24 hours notice for cancellations. Sessions cancelled with less notice may be charged.
Is the first session free?
No — the first session is not free. If you want to meet Bastian and set goals before committing to training, book a 45-minute goal-setting consultation for $50. There's no commitment required after the consultation.
