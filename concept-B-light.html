FAQ
Frequently Asked Questions
What ages do you train?

We work with athletes from ages 8 through adulthood. Youth, middle school, high school, and adult personal training are all available.

Where are sessions held?

Sessions take place at our training facility. See the Location section below for the full address and directions.

How long is each session?

Standard sessions are 60 minutes. Speed and agility sessions can be 45 or 60 minutes depending on the program.

Do you offer group or team training?

Yes — we offer group training, team-specific sessions, and clinic packages. Contact us for group rates.

What should my child bring?

Athletic clothing, sneakers (or cleats for field work), a water bottle, and any sport-specific gear (glove, bat, etc.). We provide all training equipment.

What's your cancellation policy?

We ask for at least 24 hours notice for cancellations. Sessions cancelled with less notice may be charged.

Is the first session free?

We offer a free 30-minute consultation to discuss your goals and meet Coach Bastian before you commit to anything.

Are you certified and insured?

Yes — Coach Bastian is fully insured, NSCA certified, CPR/First Aid certified, and background-check verified. See our Credentials section for details.
